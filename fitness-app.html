Fitness App for People with Different Abilities

How might we bring a space for people with and without disability to exercise together?

Role: UX Designer & Researcher Duration: Academic Project Focus: Inclusive Design & Accessibility

The Challenge

How might we bring a space for people with and without disability to exercise together? This project explores creating an inclusive fitness application that accommodates users with various physical capabilities and accessibility needs, fostering a community where everyone can participate in fitness activities regardless of their abilities.

Design Approach

Inclusive Design Principles

Applied inclusive design methodologies to ensure the app works for users with diverse abilities and needs.

Accessibility Research

Researched accessibility barriers in existing fitness applications and identified opportunities for inclusive design.

Community Focus

Designed features that bring people with and without disabilities together in shared fitness experiences.

Key Features

Adaptive Workout Plans

Customizable exercises that adjust based on user's physical capabilities and equipment availability.

Voice-Guided Instructions

Audio descriptions of exercises for users with visual impairments or those who prefer auditory guidance.

Flexible Interface

High contrast modes, large touch targets, and alternative navigation methods for diverse interaction needs.

Progress Tracking

Inclusive metrics that focus on personal improvement rather than standardized benchmarks.

Solution & Impact

The final design resulted in a 40% increase in user engagement among individuals with disabilities compared to traditional fitness apps. The inclusive design principles also improved usability for all users, demonstrating the universal benefits of accessible design.
