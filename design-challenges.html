‚ö° EV Charging Station Finder App

Challenge: Design a mobile app that helps electric vehicle drivers find, reserve, and pay for charging stations. The app must work seamlessly while driving and accommodate users with different abilities.

Key Requirements:

  • Real-time station availability and pricing
  • Route planning with range anxiety consideration
  • Hands-free operation while driving
  • Payment integration for multiple account types

Constraints: Accessible while driving, real-time data updates, offline capability, WCAG AAA compliance

Success Metrics: 60% faster charger discovery, supports 3 user types (individual, ride-share, fleet)

Development Constraints:
  • Performance: 60fps animations on iOS 12+
  • Components: Reusable Button component with 4 states
  • Accessibility: VoiceOver + Switch Control support
  • Edge Cases: Division by zero, overflow handling
My Design System Decisions:
  • Spacing: 8px grid system (thumb-optimized)
  • Typography: 32px display, 18px buttons
  • Colors: Extended my portfolio brand palette
  • Animations: 200ms press feedback (iOS-native feel)

üéØ My Design Philosophy Applied

"Inclusive design isn't an afterthought - it's the foundation."

How I applied learnings from SSS and McMaster EDI research:

  • Started with accessibility requirements, not retrofitted them
  • Prioritized motor accessibility based on my work with diverse users
  • Applied equity-driven design thinking from my workshop facilitation experience
  • Considered intersectional needs (vision + motor + cognitive simultaneously)
