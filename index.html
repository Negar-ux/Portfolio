My Journey

From engineering to product design to design thinking research - my path has been driven by a passion for creating inclusive experiences and advancing design education.

Engineering Foundation

Started with a strong technical background in engineering, developing analytical thinking and problem-solving skills that continue to inform my design approach.

Transition to Design

Discovered my passion for user-centered design and accessibility, leading me to focus on creating digital experiences that work for everyone, especially people with different abilities.

Academic Research

Expanded into design thinking research at McMaster University, exploring how inclusive design principles can transform engineering education and create more equitable learning environments.

Bridging Industry & Academia

Today, I work at the intersection of product design and educational research, applying design thinking to both digital products and academic challenges.
